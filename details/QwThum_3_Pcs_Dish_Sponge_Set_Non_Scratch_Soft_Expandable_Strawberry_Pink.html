Introducing QwThum Dish Sponge Set

Transform your cleaning routine with our stylish and functional 3-piece dish sponge set. Designed with care and quality, these sponges combine softness, durability, and charm to make kitchen tasks more enjoyable.

Soft & Non-Scratch Material

Our dish sponges are crafted with a gentle, non-scratch cellulose material that safely cleans delicate surfaces and cookware. Whether you're wiping counters or scrubbing a sink, these sponges won't leave scratches or damage your kitchenware.

Absorbent and Expandable

With an absorbent cellulose composition, these sponges expand when wet, giving you more cleaning power to easily remove stubborn stains and dirt. This makes them ideal for both heavy-duty and everyday kitchen cleaning tasks.

Strawberry Pink Design

Add a pop of color to your kitchen with our charming strawberry pink dish sponges. The vibrant design not only looks great but also makes cleaning a more pleasant and engaging activity.

Eco-Friendly & Gentle on Skin

Each sponge is made from safe, natural wood pulp and terry fabric, providing a soft and gentle clean for sensitive skin. This eco-friendly material is perfect for those who care about sustainability as well as their home's cleanliness.

Convenient Lanyard Included

The included lanyard allows you to hang your sponges for easy storage and quick drying in a well-ventilated area. This small addition goes a long way in keeping your kitchen tidy and your sponges hygienic.

Durable for Long-Lasting Use

Designed to last, these sponges are built for long-term use with proper care. Their lightweight and expandable texture ensures they remain effective even after multiple cleanings, keeping your kitchen sparkling day after day.

The Perfect Cleaning Essential

Whether you're handling kitchen or bathroom tasks, these eco-friendly, soft expandable sponges are a must-have. Combine practicality with a fun touch and elevate your daily cleaning routine with QwThum.
