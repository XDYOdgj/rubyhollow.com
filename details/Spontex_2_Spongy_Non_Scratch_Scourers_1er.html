Spontex 2 Spongy Non Scratch Scourers

Say goodbye to harsh scrubbing and hello to a smarter, eco-friendly cleaning solution. These Spontex 2 Spongy Non Scratch Scourers offer a dual-sided design, combining the softness of a sponge with a non-scratch scouring surface. Perfect for daily use, they're ideal for a variety of surfaces including countertops, dishes, and delicate materials like stainless steel and glass.

Crafted from 100% recycled fibers, these scourers are not only sustainable but also gentle on your home. Their specially contoured shape makes it easy to clean tight corners and hard-to-reach spaces, ensuring every part of your home stays spotless. The absorbent sponge side is great for wiping up spills and moisture, while the scouring side helps remove stubborn stains without causing scratches.

Whether you're refinishing your kitchen or giving your bathroom a deep clean, these versatile tools make your cleaning routine faster and more efficient. Their compact size means they're easy to store and carry, so you can keep them handy wherever you need them. With Spontex scourers, you can maintain a clean, scratch-free home with minimal effort and a positive impact on the environment.
