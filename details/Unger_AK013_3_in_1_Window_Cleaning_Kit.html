Unger AK013 3-in-1 Window Cleaning Kit

Whether you're a homeowner or a professional cleaner, the Unger AK013 is designed to make your window cleaning experience easier and more effective. This comprehensive set includes three essential tools: a high-quality squeegee, a powerful microfibre cleaner, and a durable cleaning bucket, all built to last and perform at their best.

Streak-Free Cleaning, Every Time

The 35cm ErgoTec Squeegee comes with a unique S-Channel and a quick-change rubber blade, allowing you to switch blades in seconds and tackle even the toughest stains with ease. Its ergonomic design ensures comfort and better control, making it ideal for both small and large windows.

Efficient and Powerful Cleaning Tools

The 35cm Microfibre Power Washer is paired with a T-Bar handle for stability and includes a built-in scrubbing pad to help remove stubborn dirt and grime. This powerful yet lightweight tool is perfect for deep cleaning and is suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Convenient Storage and Functionality

The included 12L cleaning bucket is designed with storage compartments, making it easy to carry all your cleaning essentials together. It also features volume markings to help you mix cleaning solutions accurately and safely. Its wringing and draining capabilities ensure efficient use and minimal mess.

Why Professionals Trust the Unger AK013

Known for its precision and durability, the Unger AK013 is trusted by professionals who demand high-quality tools. This 3-in-1 set combines reliability, ease of use, and excellent performance to deliver spotless, streak-free windows and a hassle-free cleaning experience.
